Decoding Electrochemical Interfaces, Degradation, and Corrosion

Yoon Lab develops quantitative electrochemical diagnostics to reveal hidden interfacial reactions, transport limitations, side reactions, and failure mechanisms in energy materials and devices.

Research → Publications → Join Us →
About Yoon Lab

Our research bridges electrochemical analysis, materials characterization, and interface engineering to advance batteries, solid-state electrolytes, corrosion science, supercapacitors, seawater electrolysis, and electrochemical sensors. We aim to establish mechanistic links between measured electrochemical signals and real interfacial phenomena.

Research Themes

Signal-based understanding of electrochemical systems

We combine electrochemical measurements, transient analysis, materials characterization, and modeling to decode complex interfacial and degradation processes.
